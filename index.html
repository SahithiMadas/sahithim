Data Analyst & AI Engineer with 3+ years building analytics systems, ML pipelines, and LLM-powered applications across government, enterprise, and research environments. M.S. in Data Science, University at Albany, SUNY — Dean's Merit Scholar, Graduated 2025.

My work spans the full stack — from designing ETL pipelines and time series forecasting models to shipping RAG-based AI tools and 20+ executive-facing dashboards.
